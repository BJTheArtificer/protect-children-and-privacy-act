# Sponsor Outreach Letters

This directory contains tailored letters to specific Arkansas legislators asking them to sponsor or co-sponsor the Arkansas Protects Our Children and Our Privacy Online Act.

Each letter is written for the specific representative and their stated political priorities. The letters share a common framing — that operating-system-level age verification is coming whether anyone likes it or not, and Arkansas has a choice between leading with a privacy-preserving model or being swept into California's surveillance-oriented approach — but each letter emphasizes the angles most likely to resonate with the recipient.

The letters target both Republicans and Democrats deliberately. This bill is not a partisan bill, and a bipartisan sponsor list strengthens it both legally (showing legitimate state interest across the political spectrum) and politically (broadening the base of support for floor passage).

If you are reaching out to a legislator not represented here, feel free to use any of these letters as a template. The shared framing in each letter — "the boulder is rolling, let's steer it" — is the strongest common message. Tailor the specific concerns to your legislator's record.

If you are a legislator yourself reading this and want to be added to or removed from the outreach list, please open an issue on the repository.
